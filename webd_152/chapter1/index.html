My favorite drink is a mocha from Starbucks. Not just a normal mocha though, I have to be difficult and make it a “tall hot mocha with oat milk, an extra shot of espresso, no whip”. I will say the mocha tastes way better when you get it with the normal whole fat milk and whipped cream, but these Starbucks drinks have a crazy amount of sugar and the best way I’ve found to make this a somewhat low fat/low sugar drink is to get the smallest size possible (because they add less mocha sauce than the larger sizes) and then add extra espresso, replace the milk with oat milk (no fat and much less sugar than whole milk) and remove the whipped cream.

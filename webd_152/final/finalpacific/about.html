About Pacific Trails

Opened by Joan and Michael Hawthorne in 2008, Pacific Trails Resort has been providing a luxury nature experience to patrons for 15 years now. Our goal is to bring the comfort of a bed and breakfast to the great outdoors of the beautiful Pacific Coast, allowing our guests to soak in the serenity of the area, while still enjoying our luxury amenities. We strive to offer the best of both worlds; outdoor adventures such as birdwatching, guided hikes, kayaking excursions, and sunrise yoga sessions, as well as comfort amenities like our well-appointed yurts supplied with running water and queen size beds, prepared-to-order breakfasts and complimentary appetizers and drinks in our welcoming lodge, and our heated pool and jacuzzi. Come enjoy this one-of-a-kind experience with the Pacific Trails Resort family!

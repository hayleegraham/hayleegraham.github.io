About Me

My name is Haylee Graham and I am 26 years old. I currently work full time as a Senior Audio Visual Technician at Jamul Casino. I have 10+ years of experience as an Audio Engineer, working in theater, conference, corporate, concert, and small venue settings. As an Audio Visual Technician, I have worked with video systems (cameras, switchers, playback), LED video wall displays, lights and lighting consoles, and Crestron systems. I also have experience using Dante networking systems and AV over IP knowledge.

I grew up in San Diego but I have also lived in Connecticut and Colorado. I currently live in San Diego with my boyfriend of 3 years. He is a full stack software developer, and is one of my inspirations for taking this class. I am a full time student majoring in Music Technology, and attempting a certificate in Web Development. I just started learning to code this year, and I am taking this course to enhance my HTML and CSS skills, and learn more about the web development industry.

Hiking at Mission Trails

I enjoy hiking and Mission Trails Regional Park is one of my favorite places to hike. The park offers many trails, my favorite being the Oak Canyon Trail which follows a series of waterfalls leading down to the San Diego River.

Dog Sledding

My boyfriend and I got to experience dog sledding in Canada while visiting his family in the Montreal area. It was actually quite the workout, you'd be surpised how much cardio was involved... But those dogs love to run!

Snowboarding

I recently learned how to snowboard, my first trip was to Big Bear, CA in January of 2023. On my trip to Canada in March, after 3 days of snowboarding, I learned how to do heal-side and toe-side carves!

Sailing

My boyfriend and I have been learning to sail. We have gone on many trips with his parents teaching us the basics. We usually sail from the Navy Base in Point Loma to the Coronado Bridge or to the tip of Point Loma, both about 12 mile trips.

Hiking

Hiking has become one of my favorite activities over the last few years. In 2020, I weighed over 220lbs, I am now down to 150lbs. Hiking has become my form of meditation and has helped me better myself.

One-Wheeling

This crazy device is called a One Wheel; basically a motorized skateboard that operates similar to a Segway by shifting your weight. I love cruising around the beach towns and Downtown on it, and they can really come in handy when parking is impossible.

80z All Stars - May 13th, 2023

JIVe

Review By: Haylee Graham

Review:

80z All Stars is a consistantly good band with talented musicians and crowd-pleasing song choices. Their dance moves never fail to get the guests up and dancing with them.

They play the hits of the 80's; from Billy Joel to Prince to "Hey Mickey", they do it all. The crowd loves the nostalgic music and themed outfits that the band wears.

Their choreographed dance routines are definitely the icing on the cake. They are so fun to watch on-stage, and their energy is contagious.

The band members are all very professional and easy to work with. They bring most of their own high-end equiptment, creating a high-quality sound and an easier set-up process for the AV team.

I would recommend keeping 80z All Stars in the regular rotation for the JIVe venue as they are a crowd and staff favorite.
